How to Use the Chat

Our AI-powered chat feature is designed to provide instant answers about our storage services. Follow these steps to test and use it:

  • Click the animated "?" button in the bottom right corner to open the chat window.
  • The chat will start with a welcome message and prompt you to enter your first name, last name, and email address sequentially.
  • Once your details are provided, ask questions about storage units, pricing, or availability (e.g., "What is your biggest unit?" or "Are there discounts for long-term rentals?").
  • Use the share button (share icon) to download the conversation as a PDF or send it via email. You can also type "share conversation" or "send copy" to initiate sharing.
